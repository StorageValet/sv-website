Partnership Options

Flexible engagement models

Choose the partnership level that works for your property. From simple referral to deep integration.

1

Approved Partner Amenity

Best for: Buildings testing resident interest

List Storage Valet as an approved vendor. We provide marketing materials for your amenity packet. Residents sign up directly with us at standard rates. No cost to the building.

2

Preferred Partner Plus

Best for: Buildings wanting exclusive benefits

Portal Integration

Staged integration into your resident portal. Starts with deep linking, can progress to SSO and eventually full API embedding. Residents never leave your ecosystem.

How It Works

Simple operations, premium experience

1

Resident Signs Up

2
