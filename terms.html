2. Description of Services

Storage Valet provides premium storage concierge services, including:

  • Pickup and delivery of personal belongings
  • Secure, climate-controlled storage
  • Digital inventory management
  • On-demand item retrieval and delivery

Our Services are currently available in Hudson County, New Jersey, including Jersey City, Hoboken, Weehawken, and Port Imperial.

3. Membership and Billing

Membership Fee: The standard membership fee is $299 per month, plus a one-time $99 setup fee at the time of enrollment.

Cancellation: You may cancel your membership at any time. Upon cancellation, we will arrange final delivery of all stored items at no additional charge. No refunds are provided for partial months.

Payment Methods: We accept major credit cards and debit cards. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Storage Valet reserves the right to refuse storage of any items at our discretion.

Limitation of Liability: Storage Valet's liability is limited to the lesser of the item's actual cash value or the coverage amount. We are not liable for items excluded from coverage or damage caused by factors beyond our control.

Additional Coverage: Members may request additional coverage for high-value items. Contact us for details and pricing.
