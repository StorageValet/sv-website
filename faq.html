Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Storage Valet's service.

Service & Coverage

We serve Hudson County's Gold Coast: Jersey City (including Downtown, Heights, and Newport), Hoboken, Weehawken, and Port Imperial. We're expanding to additional areas soon—sign up for our waitlist if you're outside these zones.

We offer pickups and deliveries 7 days a week, including evenings. We'll work with your schedule—just let us know what works best when you book.

We deliver within 48 hours of your online request. For most requests, we can deliver the next day. You'll receive a confirmation with your delivery window as soon as you submit your request.

Pricing & Billing

Storage & Security

Yes. Every membership includes $3,000 in insurance coverage at no extra cost. This is genuine replacement value coverage—not the limited liability you find with traditional storage facilities. Additional coverage is available upon request.

Our facility features 24/7 security monitoring, controlled access, and climate control for temperature and humidity. Your items are stored in a professional-grade environment, not a standard storage unit.

We can't store hazardous materials, perishable food, live plants or animals, firearms, or anything illegal. For fragile or high-value items (artwork, antiques, electronics), let us know so we can take extra care during handling.

How It Works

We photograph every item (or box) and upload it to your personal portal. You can browse your inventory from your phone or computer, see exactly what you have stored, and request specific items for delivery with a few clicks.
